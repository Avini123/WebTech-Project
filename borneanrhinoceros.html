Bornean Rhinoceros

The Bornean Rhinoceros inhabits all over Borneo, especially the rainforests. Currently, the species considered functionally extinct since only one female is surviving in captivity. Howeever, in 2016, another female was captured. This shows evidence that more Bornean Rhinoceros might still exist in the wild.
