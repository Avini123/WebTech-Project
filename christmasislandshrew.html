Christmas Island Shrew

The Christmas Island Shrew is an extremely rare shrew from Christmas Island. Its life span is very short, ranging from 1-2 years. This animal lives in rainforests and eats small beetles. It is unknown why the Christmas Island Shrew is functionally extinct, though scientists have theorized it could be due to diseases or competition. Currently, the Australian Government has adopted a recovery plan for this animal. For now, the future of this species remain unknown.
